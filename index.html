Humanity & Beyond.

What? How? Why?

ThirdEye foundation is a humanity organisation formed for the enrichment of the lower segments of the society by financially and mentally supporting and promoting by various student activities. However it will be a registered non-profit organisation which collects donations and various other financial aid from individuals and good hearted firms nationally and internationally. The fund and manpower of the organisation shall be used for initiating various programs like building of houses for the poor, providing financial support for education and other personal basic essentials, implementation of a dress bank, medical aids to lower sections of the society etc.

It shall be a students initiative and based purely at the college. The charity programs and initiatives will take utmost care to focus widely on the financially backward among the students of the college itself. Only a second priority shall be provided to initiatives which focuses the public outside the college family. The founder members of this initiative is of a mind to set up this initiative as a continuous program of charity and humanity acts even after our batch, which will be coordinated by the senior batch of the college and properly guided by the advisory committee of the organisation.
